Welcome to my page. I received a PhD in economics at Georgia State University in the summer of 2020. I currently work as an economist at the Environmental Protection Agency's National Center for Environmental Economics.

I research how air and water quality influence human health. My dissertation explored how coal ash water pollution impacts water resources and fetal health. I've also published work on the student health and academic benefits of improving school bus fleet pollution emissions.

Please feel free to reach out if you would like to learn more about my research or teaching! Thank you for visiting.
